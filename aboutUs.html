ABOUT US

We at CORE, value the art of Sustainability and Ethical Fashion. An endearing aspect
of the brand is the idea of crafting quality, cruelty-free products providing awareness
through the images that are showcased on them. These products showcase captures
from different realms of our surroundings, aesthetically fabricated into designer products
that would suit your style quotient, stemming from the opinion that fashion doesn't need
to bleed. Every handcrafted piece of Core is an expression of fine individuality and
meaningful fashion, after all, going green is the future and we sure are making it trendy.


BE UNIQUE, CREATE AWARENESS, BRING A CHANGE

OUR MISSION

To create a brand of more sustainable and
vegan products that radiate the impact of
doing good for the environment, workers &
animal kingdom - through the combination
of more sustainable textiles & eco-friendly
processes with classic style quality.

OUR VISION

To replace labels of outerwear products in
textiles derived from animals, harm the
environment and trusted products made
in more sustainable fabrics- including
laboratory grown and plant based faux/
vegan leather, sustainable denim,
sustainable wool alternatives.
